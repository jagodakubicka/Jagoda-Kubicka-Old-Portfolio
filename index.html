About me

I am a self-taught front end developer who's currently looking forward to start my journey in Tech industry.

I have BA in management and 3+ years of experience in marketing. I can speak advanced English and Japanese.

Why coding?

As of my marketing background, I wanted to get more into digital marketing and SEO. Because of that, I created site on Wordpress about Japanese business culture so I could test out all funcionalities in practice.
Unfortunately ( or fortunately?) right from the start I was annoyed by layout limitations.
I wanted to be in control of all colors, styles and funcionality, so I decided to learn basics of HTML and CSS ...and this is where I fell in love with coding.

I discovered that coding, planning, designing and solving logical issues gives me gratification.
Learning something new in every project became quite addictive to me. I love that there are many ways of solving issues and there are almost no limits.
My motivation comes from knowing that there is so much more to learn and improve myself!


Who am I?

If I were to describe myself in two words, then that would be disciplined and open minded.


Other than coding, I'm a big fan of Japanese culture. If not in VS Code, you can find me with a Japan-related book or planning my next trip to Japan.
For relaxation, together with my partner in crime, I'm doing yoga to stay healthy or I bake sweets to make days taste better.
